URL STRUCTURE:

URLs should consist of actual words and include your keywords. They should be structured in a way that search engines can efficiently crawl your site based solely on your URLs.

NAVIGATION STRUCTURE

Is your website easily navigable for users and search engine bots? Your navigation structure should be simple, organized, and consistent. This is pivotal not only for SEO but also for usability and conversion optimization.

DUPLICATE COPY CHECK

Duplicate content can harm your ranking power. If you’re reusing the same content on multiple pages or others are plagiarizing your content, it’s best to have it rewritten promptly.

A/B AND MULTIVARIATE TESTING

For optimal results from your SEO campaign, testing various scenarios is crucial to determine the path leading to the highest conversion rates and top rankings. Targeted A/B testing and multivariate testing yield real-time insights to guide your strategy.

BACKLINK REVIEW

Backlinks are significant, but it’s crucial to monitor the pages linking back to your website to ensure these links remain active and reputable. Poor links can negatively impact your SEO value. It’s also beneficial to assess your competitor’s backlinks to discover potential link partners and enhance your search engine rankings.

KEYWORD RESEARCH & COMPETITOR RESEARCH

Strategic keyword research is pivotal for maximizing your audience reach. When executed effectively, keyword research guides your content structure, blog posts, product descriptions, and on-site content.

ANALYTICS SETUP & REVIEW

Google Analytics empowers you to monitor your SEO progress, trends, and growth while identifying and addressing potential issues proactively. It also enables you to gauge how specific keywords influence your traffic and sales. You can set and track goals, generate reports, and understand your customer demographics using Analytics.

LINK BUILDING CAMPAIGNS

Link building enhances your website’s authority by connecting it with high-value websites. It’s not just about building links; it’s about securing the right links in terms of quantity and quality. Effective links can even direct new customers straight to your site.

SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGNS
