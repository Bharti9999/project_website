ERP Development Process

Our ERP development process involves key steps:

  1. Discovery of business needs and ERP requirements analysis
  2. Technical design, including feature set design, UX/UI considerations, and selection of a secure and scalable tech stack
  3. Project planning, encompassing vision, success measures, team composition, timelines, budget, and risk mitigation
  4. ERP development, including building projects from scratch, customizing available ERP solutions, or modernizing existing systems
  5. Quality assurance, running parallel testing with coding
  6. Integration, establishing connections between ERP modules and required systems
  7. Data migration, transferring company data to ERP from spreadsheets or existing planning systems
  8. Deployment, configuring ERP infrastructure and deploying the solution in the production environment
  9. User training, planning and designing training sessions for IT teams, conducted in-person or remotely
  10. Optional after-launch ERP services, offering continuous support and evolution of ERP software.

Types of ERP Systems: Modules and Architecture

Enterprise software facilitates data storage, processing, and management using modules that optimize processes and maintain structured databases. Modules include:

  • SCM (Supply Chain Management)
  • PLM (Product Lifecycle Management)
  • SRM (Supplier Relationship Management)
  • CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
  • Human Resources, containing employee contact information, salary, attendance, and engagement details.
